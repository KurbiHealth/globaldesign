We’re building Kurbi for you

You know it, we know it – MS doesn’t have you – but darn if it doesn’t make life tough sometimes. Everyday you’re doing amazing things despite the reality of MS. That’s why we’re building Kurbi, to help you share your story with the people that care for and about you.

The truth is that family, friends, doctors... they don’t always understand what living with MS is like. Your symptoms are coming and going at random times of the day, you’re managing confusing medication schedules and dosages, and you’re pushing yourself to be active even when your body is literally fighting against you. Yet you persevere. That’s the story we want to help you share one goal at a time.
